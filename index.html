About

World citizen, Generation Y and exchange student - currently living in South Carolina, United States of America. Born in Brazil, and moved to Germany when I was 13.

My main occupation field is web development. I worked for ~1 year on my (now abandoned) startup Oratio.io. After this, I worked for TREU digital GmbH for 1 year and 8 months as a web and game developer. If you wanna know more about my programming projects and achievements, click here.

I build computers, generally for friends, but I also build computers and recommend components for others for a very good price (since this is something I genuinely enjoy doing), and have experience with full custom watercooling loops. I am pretty decent at overclocking, reaching the top 1% (according to UserBenchmark) on overclocking the Intel i7-8700K for my personal rig. If you're interested in my PC specs, check them out! I also enjoy building robots, for example robots based on the Arduino platform.

I am a crypto currency enthusiast and have a crypto portfolio and mining operation running, however most of my investments are in ETFs. My portfolio consists mainly of high-tech industry and climate change.

Politically liberal in the European sense, active at Junge Liberale (Young Liberals), Freie Demokratische Partei and identify with the goals of the Alliance of Liberals and Democrats for Europe. More info on this here (in German).

If there are any questions left, please, feel free to send me an email!

Web & Game Development

Know quite a bit about Node.js, Unity-based VR and PHP.

I am mainly a full-stack web developer, I have lots of experience with the Node.js ecosystem, having built many solutions on it - my biggest project involved creating a (now abandoned) virtualization system for handling different servers on different subdomains on the same server.

I started programming with 11, when my aunt, who works at a Brazilian bank and is responsible for the database communications there, first showed me programming. I was fascinated by it and over the years got pretty good at it.

I worked with PHP/MySQL professionally for over 2 years, mainly for TREU. I worked at many projects during the time, including a VR game for engaging young people at job markets and rebuilding the website for the local Child Protection Agency.
